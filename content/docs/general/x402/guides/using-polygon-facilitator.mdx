---
title: "Using the Polygon Facilitator"
description: "How to use the Polygon Facilitator to settle x402 payments"
---

The Polygon Facilitator is available at
[x402.polygon.technology](https://x402.polygon.technology) and its health
endpoint at
[https://x402.polygon.technology/health](https://x402.polygon.technology/health).

Follow the instructions in the
[x402 Quickstart for Sellers](./quickstart-sellers.mdx) to add its address to
your API, or use the
[x402facilitators package](https://github.com/Swader/x402facilitators/) to
automatically add it to your API, or to have it as one of the available
auto-routed ones.

A detailed guide on how to use the x402facilitators package is available at
[Using the x402facilitators package](/general/x402/guides/using-x402facilitators/).
